What is the Digital Service?

The Digital Service team brings the full force of its experience and passion to transform the way technology at VA works for Veterans and their families.

We are a team of seasoned engineers, designers, and product managers from some of the best-known companies in the private sector. We are joining forces with talented VA employees to make using VA services as simple as buying a book online.

To achieve this we will:

  • Have the courage to tackle the hard problems
  • Make decisions based on the needs of our users, and data
  • Build simple and intuitive products
  • Enable modern, reliable, adaptable, and sustainable solutions

Our work is currently focused on:

  1. Improving claims processing to give Veterans the timely support they deserve.
  2. Simplifying access, sharing, and use of health information for Veterans and their doctors.
  3. Building the infrastructure that powers a unified end-to-end experience with VA, through which our customers can meet all their needs.
